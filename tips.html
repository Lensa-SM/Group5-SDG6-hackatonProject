Water Sanitation Tips

  • ✅ Always boil water before drinking if you're unsure of its quality.
  • ✅ Use water filters or chlorine tablets to remove contaminants.
  • ✅ Keep water storage containers clean and covered.
  • ✅ Avoid dumping waste near water sources.
  • ✅ Wash hands before collecting or using water.
  • ✅ Regularly test water for pH and turbidity if you're using untreated sources.
  • ✅ Raise awareness in your community about clean water practices.

About Safesip

Safesip is a simple web platform that allows users to log and monitor water sample measurements including pH level and turbidity. It helps raise awareness about clean water and supports communities in maintaining water safety.

UNDP and SDG 6

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) supports the achievement of SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation, which focuses on ensuring access to clean water and sanitation for all. UNDP invests in infrastructure, governance, and awareness to improve water resource management and reduce waterborne diseases.

NGOs Working on Water Treatment in Ethiopia

  • WaterAid Ethiopia – A global NGO working to ensure access to clean water, hygiene, and sanitation services across rural and underserved communities. Their programs promote sustainable and community-led solutions.
  • IRC Ethiopia – IRC helps build strong water, sanitation, and hygiene systems. They work with governments and local partners to ensure long-term water services and better WASH policies.
  • CARE Ethiopia – CARE supports clean water projects, especially in drought-prone areas. They focus on water access, hygiene promotion, and resilience building among vulnerable populations.
